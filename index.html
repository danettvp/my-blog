Week 01

1. What went well

This week wasn't exactly smooth, but it was still productive in its own way. I started off with quite a narrow focus, which ended up feeling a bit limiting. But that process helped me realise I needed to zoom out and take a broader look at the topic again. Once I did that, I was able to bring in earlier research and start connecting ideas in a more meaningful way. It wasn't linear, but by the end of the week I felt like I was finally moving in a clearer direction.

2. What didn't go well

I think I restricted myself too much at the start, trying to force clarity too early. That made it harder to explore freely and led to some frustration. I also noticed that I was second-guessing my ideas instead of letting them develop naturally. Not having a structured way to reflect on or track evolving thoughts probably made that worse.

3. What can be improved

One thing I've learned is that it's okay to step back and reassess â€” even if it feels like I'm going backwards. It actually helped me use my earlier research in a more thoughtful way. Going forward, I want to give myself more space to explore without over-committing too early. Having some kind of lightweight reflection or synthesis method during the week would probably help too.

4. What actions will I take for the next sprint

Next sprint, I'm going to allow more time for broad exploration upfront, then narrow down more intentionally. I'll also keep notes on how my ideas are shifting, so I can reflect as I go rather than waiting until I feel stuck. That way I can make better use of what I've already learned while still staying open to new directions.
